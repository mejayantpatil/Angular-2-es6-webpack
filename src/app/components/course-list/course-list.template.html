A Warm Welcome to TurbineTut!

TurbineTut is going to be an online learning platform. This is going to offer courses in the field of Turbines (Steam and Gas Turbines), Oil Refinery, Boilers and related Technology. Users (Customers) can enroll for these courses and get knowledge, get certified in the technology they are interested in. For providing virtual experience of inside of a say Oil Refinery, website is going to make use of Virtual Reality.
